Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our design subscription services.

How does the design subscription work?

Our design subscription allows you to submit unlimited design requests. We work on one request at a time (Starter), two requests (Professional), or three requests (Enterprise) simultaneously. Each request includes unlimited revisions until you're 100% satisfied.

What types of designs can you create?

We create everything from logos, websites, and mobile apps to marketing materials, social media graphics, and internal business tools. Our AI-powered approach ensures high-quality, consistent designs across all platforms.

How fast can you deliver designs?

Our average turnaround time is 2-3 business days for Starter plans, 1-2 business days for Professional plans, and priority scheduling for Enterprise clients. Rush requests are available for urgent projects.

Can I pause or cancel my subscription?

Yes! You can pause or cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties. Your designs and files remain yours forever, and you can reactivate your subscription whenever you need us again.

Do you offer refunds?

We do not offer refunds for our services. Once work has commenced or a subscription has been activated, no refunds will be provided. We ensure quality delivery and unlimited revisions to guarantee your satisfaction with our work.

What if I'm not happy with a design?

We offer unlimited revisions on every project until you're completely satisfied. If you're still not happy after multiple rounds, we'll start fresh or credit your account - your satisfaction is guaranteed.

Can you work with my existing brand guidelines?

Yes! We'll study your existing brand guidelines and ensure all designs maintain consistency with your current brand identity. We can also help evolve and improve your brand guidelines.

Do you offer one-off projects?

Yes! While our subscription model offers the best value, we also provide one-off project quotes for specific needs. Contact us to discuss your project requirements and get a custom quote.
